Our Mission

Water is crucial for life to exist. According to the United Nations, every individual has the right to have access to sufficient water for personal and domestic uses, which must be safe, acceptable, and affordable. However, as the global population continues to increase, so does the demand of clean and purified water. Our mission is to purify water and make it more accessible to third-world countries. So, our team founded FilTier, an efficient filter system that removes unwanted impurities from water such as, sediment, taste, odor, hardness, bacteria, etc.
